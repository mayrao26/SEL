What is Self-Management?
 

Self-management is the ability to successfully regulate one’s emotions, thoughts, and behaviors in different situations — effectively managing stress, controlling impulses, and motivating oneself. Self-management is also the ability to set and work toward personal and academic goals.

Self-Management Skills
  • Impulse control
  • Stress management
  • Self-discipline
  • Self-motivation
  • Goal-setting
  • Organizational skills
Tips for Teaching Self-Management Skills

Self-management can be taught in the form of sharing identified practices like stress management or making thought processes transparent by modeling inner dialogue, that showcases the use of self-management. For example, a teacher could lead a discussion on potentially stressful situations a student might encounter in an academic setting, then have students role play to model strategies for stress management.
